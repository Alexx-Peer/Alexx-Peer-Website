I’m Alexx—a mixologist by trade and a coder by passion.

By day (and often night), I’ve spent the last decade behind the bar crafting cocktails and curating experiences at parties, events, and private gatherings. I love the energy, the creativity, and the human connection that comes with mixology—but in recent years, I’ve also found a new creative outlet in code. I’m an amateur developer currently diving deep into Python, with a growing appreciation for web development. I’ve been building up my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—learning how to make the web not just work, but look and feel great. I enjoy the problem-solving and tinkering that comes with front-end development. When I’m not mixing drinks or writing code, I’m usually out on a trail with my Corgi, Calypso, she's old. She’s been my sidekick for eight years and is always up for an adventure, whether it’s a hike, a road trip, or just exploring a new park. I’m also an avid Dungeons & Dragons fan—I host regular campaigns and spend far too much time painting miniatures to bring the fantasy to life. Coding, cocktails, and critical hits!—that’s my vibe. I’m always looking to learn more, meet fellow creatives, and take on new challenges, whether it’s in the bar, on the page, or behind a keyboard.

Hobbies

  • Bartending

    • Bartending is more than just a job for me—it’s a creative outlet. I’ve spent over a decade mixing drinks professionally, and I still enjoy bartending private events on the side. Crafting cocktails is part science, part art, and I love experimenting with new ingredients and flavor profiles. It’s also a great way to meet people and bring a little flair and fun to any gathering.

  • Painting Miniatures

    • Painting miniatures is my quiet, focused hobby and fine-art outlet. I mostly paint figures for the Dungeons & Dragons campaigns I run, and I really enjoy the process of bringing each character to life with color and detail. It’s both relaxing and rewarding, and it adds a personal touch to my games. Plus, it’s a fun excuse to dive into lore and storytelling through tiny paintbrushes.

  • Bike Riding

    • Biking is how I reset. I like getting out into nature, and pushing myself with new paths and terrain. Whether it’s a long peaceful ride or a tough uphill grind, biking helps me stay active and gives me a chance to explore new places—sometimes with Calypso, trotting alongside.
